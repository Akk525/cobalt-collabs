What is Cobalt?

Cobalt strives to bridge the gap between students and student-led organizations. Cobalt isn't just a platform; it's a community of students that are seeking extracurricular activities, and has a vast range of organizations that are looking for volunteers or members.


Diving for opportunities? Or searching for passionate members to join your initiative? Cobalt can help.

For Students

  • Easy access to student-led organizations

  • A wide range of extracurriculars and activities offered

  • Forum for talking to like-minded students, who are passionate about the same things as you

For Organizations

  • Connect easily with students who are looking for opportunities

  • Find other organizations to partner and collaborate with

  • Grow your audience and readership by marketing it on Cobalt
