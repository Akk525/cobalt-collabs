About Us

Aiding our fellow students through the storm.

Cobalt is a site for you to discover and explore opportunities.
Meet people through projects, find volunteers to help out the underprivileged,
and work on a good cause with others. You can find projects you would like to
support on our site, and even share your own project, asking for donations or
help.

Our Story

In our journey to develop extracurricular activities we noticed there is a lot wrong with the current system; students often struggle to find extracurricular opportunities while organisations are starved for members. To us the problem was rather simple: we connect the two. And thats how Cobalt was founded. We wanted to create a website to make extracurriculars easier to navigate, find and explore.
